Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Looksmaxxing ("we", "us", the "App") collects, uses and protects your information. We built Looksmaxxing to be private by default: there is no account to create, and your data stays on your device except when it must be processed to generate your analysis. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

The short version: Your photos are used only to generate your facial analysis and plan. They are stored on your device. We do not sell your data, and we do not require you to create an account.

1. Information we collect

Photos you provide

To analyze your face, the App uses selfies you capture or choose (typically a front and side photo), and any optional progress photos you add over time.

Information you enter

During onboarding you may provide details such as your age, gender, goals, style preferences, hair length, skin type and concerns, and sleep habits. This information is used to personalize your analysis and 30-day plan.

Purchase information

If you buy a subscription or other in-app purchase, the transaction is handled by Apple and our payment infrastructure provider. We receive your subscription status, but we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Technical & usage information

Like most apps, we may process limited technical information (such as device type, app version, language and diagnostic/crash information) to keep the App working reliably.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate your AI facial analysis, score, feature breakdown and perceived-age estimate;
  • To create and adapt your personalized 30-day plan;
  • To track your progress over time (score trends, before/after, streaks);
  • To power the in-app AI coach with advice grounded in your own results;
  • To process and restore purchases and subscriptions;
  • To maintain, secure, debug and improve the App.

3. How the AI analysis works

Facial analysis, plan generation and the AI coach are powered by third-party generative-AI services (Google's Firebase AI / Vertex AI, using Gemini models). To produce your results, your photos and relevant inputs are transmitted securely to these services for processing and the results are returned to the App.

These requests are made to provide the service to you. We do not use your photos to train our own models, and we do not authorize the AI provider to use your content to train their general models under the enterprise terms we operate under. Processing by the AI provider is also governed by its own terms and privacy policies.

4. Where your data is stored

Your photos, onboarding answers, results and plan are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or a central profile database of your personal analysis. Photos are transmitted to the AI provider only for the moment of processing, as described above.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers necessary to run the App, and only for that purpose:

  • Google (Firebase AI / Vertex AI / Gemini) — to process your photos and inputs and return your analysis, plan and coach responses;
  • RevenueCat — to manage and validate subscriptions and in-app purchases;
  • Apple — to process payments and distribute the App.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety and security of our users or the App.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your data: You can remove your data at any time by using the "Start over" option in the App, and/or by deleting the App from your device, which removes locally-stored data.
  • Manage subscriptions: You can view, change or cancel a subscription in your Apple account settings (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions).
  • Requests: Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data. Contact us and we will respond as required by applicable law.

7. Data retention

Because your content is primarily stored on your device, you control its retention. Deleting the App or using "Start over" removes locally-stored data. Purchase records held by Apple and our payment provider are retained according to their policies.

8. Children's privacy

Looksmaxxing is intended for users aged 17 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission of photos to the AI provider. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International users

Our service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Where we transfer information, we rely on appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" above. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data? Email us at support@looksmaxxings.app.
